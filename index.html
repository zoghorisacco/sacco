    SHARE CAPITAL

    Money invested in the company as equity which forms part of the sacco's core capital,represents each member's ownership of the Sacco and earns the shareholders dividends at the end of every financial year

    Read More

    DEPOSIT CONTRIBUTION

    This is the monthly deposit made by a member against which they take loans, guarantee loans and earn interest/rebate at the end of the year.

    Read More

    LOANS

    We extend very affordable loan facilities to our members and clients on very affordable terms of interest rates. Our rates are all based on reducing balances equal instalments models.

    Read More

    LOAN REFINANCING

    Refinancing simply allows you to replace the former loan with a newer loan that pays off the former, with different loan terms and conditions. In simple terms, this topping up your initial loan such that the refinanced loan allows you to have more money.

    Read More

    LOAN RESTRUCTURING

    We allow our clients to elongate the loan terms/period to lessen the burden of monthly repayment instalments

    Read More

    ASSET FINANCING

    We do extend credit to our clients and members to allow them make orders and purchases of assets as long as their non-withdrawable deposits allow them to acquire such an asset on credit.
